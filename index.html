    about us

    Our Story

    From the time when we were tugging on our Moroccan grandmother’s apron, we learned the traditions and care put into every meal experienced in the feasts with our families and relatives.

     

    Over the years we have interwoven Mediterranean flavors and hosted parties and events for our family and friends, where over time we were encouraged to offer our menus to a wider audience. We now cater parties and events bringing the care that we infuse in every dish to your friends and family.

    ​

    Our catering service is dedicated to bringing the best of both worlds to your table, using a combination of traditional Moroccan recipes fused with fresh Mediterranean ingredients. From the vegetables and fruits from the Mediterranean garden to rich flavourful meats, our dishes are a delight to the taste and eye.

    Our focus on using fresh and basic ingredients ensures that our food is not only delicious but also healthy and nutritious. We take pride in creating dishes that are not only satisfying for the palate but also nourishing for the body based on the traditions of Morocco.

     

    Our catering service is perfect for those who want to enjoy a delicious and wholesome meal with their family and friends in the comfort of their own home. We believe that food should be a source of joy and comfort, and we strive to bring that same feeling to your table with our homemade dishes.

     

    So come and experience the taste of Morocco and the Mediterranean with us, and let us take you on a culinary journey that will leave you wanting more.
